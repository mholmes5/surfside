Surfside Golf and Beach Resort

Surfside Golf and Beach Resort features beautiful 1, 2, and 3 bedroom condos and great views of the Gulf. Come and relax on the beach, dine at one of our restaurants, take a dip in our beachside pools, or play a round on one of our championship golf courses.

Go for a swim, paddleboard, kayak or snorkel in the emerald green waters or just hang out on our 1100 feet of white sand beaches. Build a sand castle or go crabbing.

Spanning the main highway loaded with award-winning restaurants, shopping, and other great family attractions. We stretch from the Gulf of Mexico to Seminole Bay packed with condos, hotels, golf courses, Bayside Village and its excellent night life and award winning restaurants, and many more amenities.

Plan your visit today and see why so many make Surfside their choice year after year for summertime fun and relaxation on the Gulf Coast!

Great Views

Picturesque views abound at Surfside. From the balconies of our beachside condominiums and hotels, you can see the inviting Gulf waters and sugary white sand beaches. Our bayside condos and villas feature views of the peaceful and tranquil waters of Seminole Bay or the exciting nightlife of Baytowne Village.

Championship Golf

Surfside features four championship golf courses spread across the resort. Our courses wind their way through both sides of the resort. Play along Seminole Bay or amid natural beauty of the area. Book your tee time today
